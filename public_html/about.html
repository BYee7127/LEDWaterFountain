Built in 1998, Asian Star Restaurant first debuted as a part of the Canyon Racquet Club in Cottonwood Heights, Utah. In 2006, the restaurant moved into a brand new building in Sandy (or Midvale). By 2019, the restaurant has been in operation for just over 20 years and is run by people with over 25 years of experience. Customers can be certain they are getting quality food and service, so what are you waiting for? Come on in and enjoy some good Chinese food!
