Hello! My name is Beverly Yee, as you have probably guessed by now. I am currently a sophomore at the University of Utah, working towards a Computer Science major with an EAE emphasis and a Computer Engineering major. I am 19 years old and I am a second generation Chinese American. My dad came to the US in the late 1980's and then settled in Salt Lake City in the early 1990's, where he then proceeded to bring my mom here. I have one younger brother and live with my family and paternal grandmother.

My hobbies include: updating my personal website, drawing, reading manga, watching anime, writing, listening to music and some other things that I don't remember off the top of my head. A lot of the stuff that I've drawn or written are on my personal website and you are more than welcome to check it out (though it may be a little outdated right now)!

By the way, don't take my quote seriously, if it's wrong. My language skills are quite poor in both Mandarin and Japanese. :)
